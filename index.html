Opaque Magazine

A magazine about music and sleep. It simply explores the connection between these two things. Contents include music reviews of relevant musicians, interviews of different people about their sleeps and things around, a mixtape and a photography feature. My magazine concentrates on artists whose music is quite soft and mellow, overall easy-to-sleep-to kind. For interviews, questions are just simple things like what music they listen to before sleeping, where do they sleep best, so on. There is also a photography article which was done by me, and a mixtape.

Ukiyo

Ukiyo is a modern tea house in Tokyo, Japan. 'Ukiyo' in Japanese means 'The Floating World'. The logo design resembles this notion and mint green is used as the main color for a tranquil and delicate brand visual.

Greenwood

Greenwood is a small handmade clothing brand based in Hanoi, Vietnam. The logo focuses on simplicity and freshness.
